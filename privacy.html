3. Video Content Pipeline

The Video Content Pipeline is a content automation tool developed by Richard Abreu Studio. When you use this tool, it may access the following services on your behalf:

  • ElevenLabs API: To generate voice-cloned audio narration.

OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers. You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account permissions.
