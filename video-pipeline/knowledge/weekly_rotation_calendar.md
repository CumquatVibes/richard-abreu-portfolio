# Weekly Channel Rotation Calendar

**Purpose:** Focus production on 4 channels per week to maintain quality and consistency.
Each week, all effort (scripts, audio, B-roll, video assembly, uploads, optimization) targets only these 4 channels.

**Cadence per channel per week:**
- 2x long-form videos
- 3x Shorts
- Thumbnail + SEO optimization pass
- Analytics review from prior content

## How to Use This Calendar

1. **Check the current week** — find which 4 channels are in focus
2. **Record voiceovers** for those 4 channels' scripts (from `output/scripts_to_record/{Channel}/`)
3. **Drop audio files** into `output/audio/` with matching filenames (.mp3)
4. **Pipeline assembles videos** automatically once audio + B-roll exist
5. **Upload happens** via nightly pipeline (6/day quota limit)
6. **Review analytics** from the previous rotation's channels
7. **Move to next week**
